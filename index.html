About .me

I am a PhD Student at Dipartimento di Elettronica, Informazione e Bioingegneria (DEIB), Politecnico di Milano in Italy. I am currently working at the “Novel and Emerging Computing System Technologies” Laboratory (NECST Lab) with Prof. Marco Santambrogio. My research interests revolve around ubiquitous computing, mobile and pervasive systems.

In late 2012, I founded Lakeside Srl, an innovative start-up in which I serve as Chief Technology Officer (CTO) for the project Afterclaps.
